the cafeteria

Be aware - the food may be yummy but the mold isn't

food description
Pizza Looks good from the front but when u turn it over it is RAW - ok to eat sometimes if you are very hungry
Long line food Probably the best food they have in the cafeteria tbh (especially the nachos and the noodles omg) but the line is not worth it
Pasta line Pretty good but gets boring pretty quickly. Don't want to know what the sauce really has in it.
Fries Tastes good but again I have found mold on it which makes it scary (still eat it though)
Chips overpriced but tastes good and they have a good selection
COOKIES actually the best thing to get in the cafeteria - every flavor, every cookie, every time tastes amazing.
muffins my beloved idk what they put in that banana muffin but i actually think it might be crack. as a rule of thumb, the stuff that the caf doesn't make tends to be better
